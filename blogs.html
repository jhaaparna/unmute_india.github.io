8 Side Hustles You Can Start While Studying.


January 15 2022 2:03PM IST | BY : Aparna Jha


Do you struggle with finding a job or money? Have you thought about starting your own side hustle? Do you have a large project that is more enjoyable than a normal full time job? If your answer to any of these questions is yes, then you might want to consider your options.

Many college students are attempting to balance their studies with making money. Although this is all new to many college students, it doesn't have to be. Some college students are taking advantage of the internet to make extra money on the side, while also maintaining the life they currently have.

Some of the websites that you can use to start your side hustle are created to help you meet other people to start side hustles with, while also connecting with people who are looking for projects to complete.

If you are looking for a new job that you can do while in school, you can search online for opportunities that need your skills.


1. Translator Service

In today's time, knowledge of all languages should be there. People do not have enough time to learn all the languages. If you have knowledge of many languages, then this will be a icing on the cake for you. You can start the work of translator service. People who need you will contact you. In today's time, translating service is in trend and there is good income in it.



2. Freelancer

Online tutor business also gives a lot of benefits in today's time. After Covid- 19, online education has become more trend. In today's internet age, the education system completely shifted online. This is the best way for college, school student for side business.



3. Online Tutor

More and more people are making their living as freelancers who are creators of website content. If you have a passion for writing well then you should write content. Upwork and Fiverr are websites that connect freelancers with people who need to work. Writers can apply for each project individually, choose their rates and work to complete the project on their own time.

If you are fond of traveling, then there is a better chance for college students, you can earn good money by uploading videos, vlogs, giving good content in it to open YouTube channel.



6. Sell online product

In today's time everyone uses phone and social media. Why not earn money by selling stuff on social media? There are many apps, which give the opportunity to the users to make income without any investment. Meesho is a shopping app from which you can buy and sell clothes, utensils, jewelry, etc. That too by putting a price on its own.



7. Logo Design

Everyone wants to start their own business. Along with investment, planning is also very important to start a business. To grow the business. For college, school children who have a special interest in graphic design. They can do logo design work as a part time job.



8. Research Assistant

Research is not just about missiles. We need research to write anything. In today's time, there is a special demand for it, by doing research, analysis on any topic. You can also earn on hourly basis.
