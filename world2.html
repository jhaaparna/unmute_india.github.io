Iran's leader's website shows animation video of drone attack on Donald Trump


January 15 2022 4:03PM IST | By : Aparna Jha


The website of Iran's supreme leader showed an animated video showing a robot calling in a drone strike to assassinate former US President Donald Trump. The animated video was part of a contest to mark the January 3, 2020, killing of Iranian General Qasem Soleimani, who was killed in a US drone strike in Baghdad. Video posted Wednesday on Khamenei's office website appears to target Trump in a drone attack on his golf course in Mar-a-Lago, Florida. The video featured a propaganda poster last year showing Trump on a golf course, calling for revenge for Soleimani's murder. Earlier this month, Iran's hardline President Ibrahim Raisi called for Trump to be "prosecuted and killed". If not, I am telling all American leaders, do not doubt that the hand of revenge will slip out of the sleeve of the Ummah," Raisi said, referring to the worldwide community of Muslims.
